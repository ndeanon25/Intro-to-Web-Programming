FILIPINO SINIGANG

This is one of my favoirte dishes that my mom used to make. It is a filipino sour soup dish. It is filled with veggies and hearty meats.

Equipment

  • Pot
  • Large Mixing bowl
  • Cutting Board
  • Tongs
  • Fork
  • Scale
  • Small bowls

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Tbsp of oil of your choice
  • 3lbs pork spareribs, cubed
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 large tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 8ox(pack) frozen kamias
  • 6 cups of water
  • 4 pieces taro, peeled and halved
  • 1 bunch of bokchoy
  • fish sauce

PREPERATION

  1. Cut the spareribs into cubes
  2. Cut the onion into sliced
  3. Cut the three tomatoes into quarters
  4. Peel the 4 taros and cut them in half.
  5. Wash the bokchoy and cut the stem off.

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a pot over medium heat, add oil and sear pork ribs, 1-2 minutes each side. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, add onion and tomato. Saute for 3-4 minutes until onions begin to brown.
  3. Add frozen kamias and return pork ribs to the pot. Add water and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat, cover and cook for 60 minutes until pork is fork tender.
  5. Add taro and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add bokchoy and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.

SERVING SUGGESTIONS

Serve with a side of rice. Enjoy!
